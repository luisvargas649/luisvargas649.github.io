Our Vision

Settlements

Since the beginning, we been interested on creating settlements through the verse. Each settlement will be considered a District and focus on at least one profession and assist the others.

Military

The military also known as District One is going to focus on protecting all the Districts and our interests. 

Trade

We cannot do everything alone. Trade partners are an important aspect of our organization. We will trade with anyone who wants to enrich themselves.

Diplomacy

Why diplomacy? Because we can't fight everyone by ourselves.

Community

A community that works together, sticks together. We plan to have various community events to keep everyone engage. This could range from duels to races to anything crazy we can think about.

Why Join?

Because you have nothing to lose and a lot to gain. With us, you have the opportunity of being there since the start. Together we can make a profit and buy badass ships. After all, we are just a group of people who want to have fun and make new friends.
